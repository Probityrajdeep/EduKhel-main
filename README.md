    <h2>📌 Overview</h2>
    <p>Welcome to the <strong>EduKhel Website</strong> repository! 🚀 EduKhel is India’s first AI-powered
        <strong>gamified learning platform</strong> designed to make education fun, interactive, and accessible for students.  
        Our mission is to combine education, gaming, and community in a clean, responsive interface accessible on any device 💻📱.</p>

    <h2>🌟 Features</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>✅ Gamified Learning – Study Math, Science, and History as quests, challenges, and battles.</li>
        <li>✅ AI-Powered Personalization – Adaptive difficulty that adjusts to every student’s learning speed.</li>
        <li>✅ Reward System – Earn coins, badges, and rise in leaderboards.</li>
        <li>✅ Community Arena – Challenge friends in quiz battles and tournaments.</li>
        <li>✅ Voice + Visual Learning – Interactive animations and narrated lessons.</li>
        <li>✅ Multi-Language Support – English, Hindi, and regional languages.</li>
        <li>✅ Offline Mode – Designed for rural students with limited internet access.</li>
        <li>✅ Teacher Dashboard – For teachers/parents to track student progress.</li>
    </ul>
